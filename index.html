SARS

Medical Cause

SARS can spread when a person touches something that contains infected fluid or touch a infected human. SARS can also spread through air.

Social Cause

People come closer to help when someone get sick.

People are crowded, they eats a lot meat.


Political Cause


The government put the “stop SARS” poster 

everywhere, they build more hospitals and tell 

everyone to wear the a mask.




Geographic location/cause


Too many cars, too many buildings and 


factories.


Economic Cause
Companies that sells meat lose a lot of money, because the government
told everyone to not eat meat.
